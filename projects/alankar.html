Alankar Website

Overview

A dynamic and visually appealing wordpress website designed to showcase the shop's extensive range of tiles, flooring, sinks, kitchen appliances, washbasins, and other bath essentials.

Screenshots

Home Page

The homepage of the shop website is crafted to showcase an extensive range of tiles, flooring, sinks, kitchen appliances, washbasins, and other bath essentials. It provides a visually appealing layout with easy navigation to explore product categories and detailed offerings.

Products Page

The Products page is organized into distinct categories, including Modular Kitchen, Sanitaryware, Wellness, Lighting, and Tiles. Each category features a curated selection of products, allowing users to explore and find exactly what they need with ease.

About Page

The About page provides an insightful overview of the shop, detailing its mission, values, and commitment to offering high-quality products along with its inspiration gallery. It highlights the shop's expertise in delivering top-notch solutions for home and bathroom design, ensuring customer satisfaction and trust.

Contact Page

The Contact page offers users multiple ways to get in touch, including a contact form, phone number, and email. Additionally, it features a site map for easy navigation, helping visitors quickly locate important sections of the website.
